About Us

Karatu Safari Camp Lodge - Since 2018

Welcome to Karatu Safari Camp Lodge , a family owned property that was established in 2018 by Mr. Dunstan and his family. With over 10 years of experience working in hospitality industry in Tanzania, including as chief at Serengeti Safari Camp, Kilimanjaro Hostel, and New Arusha hotel, as well as an operation manager at Nomad Safaris Tanzania, Mr. Dunstan has a wealth of knowledge and expertise that he brings to his dream of owning and running a lodge.

Karatu Safari Camp lodge is the culmination of Mr, Dunstan's vision to create a warm and inviting home-way-from-home for visitors to Tanzania. Located at the heart of Karatu, a charming town situated between Lake Manyara National Park and the Ngorongoro Conservation Area, our lodge is the perfect base for exploring the stunning natural beauty of this region.

At Karatu Safari Camp Lodge, we pride ourselves on providing Exceptional service and personalized attention to each and every guest. Our lodge features comfortable and spacious accommodations, each decorated in unique style that reflectss the rich culture and heritage of Tanzania.

We offer a range of activities and experiences for guests to enjoy, including game drives, guided hikes, cultural tours and much more. Our Staff is always on hand to provide recommendations and assitance in planning your perfect safari adventure.

Why book with Karatu Safari Camp Lodge?.

5 GREAT REASONS TO BOOK WITH KARATU SAFARI CAMP LODGE.

  1. Location: The lodge is located in Karatu, a town situated between the Ngorongoro Conservation Area, Tarangire National Park, Lake Eyasi, Elephant Cave and Lake Manyara National Park. This strategic location allows guests to easily access these two popular safari destinations and enjoy the abundance of wildlife in the area.

  2. Accommodation: The lodge offers comfortable and spacious tented rooms that are designed to provide a unique and authenic safari experience. Each room has a private veranda that overlooks the beautiful natural surroundings.

  3. Amenities: Karatu Safari camp lodge offers a range of Amenities including a lounge, bar and restaurant. Guests can relax at our garden after a day of game drives or indulge in a rejuvenating spa treatment.

  4. Safari Experience: The lodge offers a variety of safari packages that cater to different interests and budgets. Experienced guides take guests on game drives in open-sided safari vehicles to explore thw wildlife-rich nearby.

  5. Conservation: The lodge is committed to conservation efforts in area and actively supports the local community through various initiatives. By choosing Karatu Safari Camp Lodge,you can be assured that you are contributing to sustainable tourism and conservation efforts in Tanzania.

Karatu Safari Camp Lodge offers a unique safari experience with comfortable accommodation, excellent amenities and a commitment to conservation. It's an ideal choice for anyone looking for an unforgettable safari adventure in Tanzania.

Our Mission

At our lodge, our mission is to provide an unforgettable wilderness experience that fosters a deep connection with nature and promotes a sense of community among our guests.

Our aim is to inspire and educate our guests about the beauty and importance of the natural world, and to create memories that will last a lifetime.
