About Us

About Karatu Safari Camp Lodge

Karatu Safari Camp Lodge is renowned for its exceptional hospitality, providing guests with an unforgettable experience in the heart of the northern Tanzania safari circuit. Nestled amidst the picturesque rolling countryside, our lodge is a sanctuary of comfort and natural beauty. Mature trees and lush gardens surround the lodge, creating a serene atmosphere where a variety of birds find their home, delighting visitors with their melodies.

Our lodge is more than just a place to rest your head; it's a tranquil and shady oasis, the perfect base for your wildlife safaris. Whether you're embarking on a thrilling game drive in the nearby national parks or simply unwinding in the peaceful ambiance of our lodge, you'll find that every moment here is infused with African hospitality.

At Karatu Safari Camp Lodge, spaciousness is a hallmark of our design, ensuring that guests have plenty of room to relax and rejuvenate. Our friendly staff are dedicated to making your stay memorable, catering to your needs with warmth and a genuine desire to share the wonders of Tanzania with you.
