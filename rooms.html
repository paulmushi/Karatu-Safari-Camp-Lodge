Rooms

Single Rooms at our Lodge

Karatu Safari Camp Lodge offers comfortable and cozy single rooms that are perfect for solo travelers or those who prefer privacy. Each room is designed with your comfort in mind and comes fully equipped with all the amenities you need for a relaxing stay.

The rooms are tastefully furnished with comfortable beds, clean linens, and warm blankets to ensure a good night's sleep. They are also equipped with en-suite bathrooms that feature hot showers, flushing toilets, and fresh towels for your convenience.

As a guest at Karatu Safari Camp Lodge, you'll enjoy full board, which means all your meals are included in the price of your stay. The lodge's restaurant serves a variety of delicious local and international cuisine, prepared using fresh ingredients and cooked to perfection.

For only $80 per person, you can experience the comfort and convenience of staying in a single room at Karatu Safari Camp Lodge. Book your stay today and discover all the beauty and excitement that Tanzania has to offer.
